Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Up Cloud. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our unified cloud storage manager application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Up Cloud acts as an interface to your existing cloud storage accounts. We collect and process the following information:

  • Authentication Information: OAuth tokens required to authenticate with Microsoft OneDrive and Google Drive.
  • Cloud Data: File metadata (names, sizes, types) and file contents retrieved strictly for the purpose of displaying and managing them within the App.
  • Local Data: Settings and preferences stored locally on your device to enhance your user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to provide the core functionality of Up Cloud:

  • To authenticate you with your selected cloud storage providers.
  • To retrieve, display, and manage your files (upload, download, delete, move) as explicitly directed by you.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any other unrelated purposes.

4. Data Storage and Transfer

Up Cloud is a local client application. Your files and authentication tokens are stored locally on your device and are communicated directly and securely (via HTTPS) between your device and the respective cloud providers (Microsoft and Google). We do not store your files or tokens on any remote Up Cloud servers.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Up Cloud's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, we only access Google Drive to manage your files as requested by you, and we do not share this data with third parties.

6. Microsoft API Terms

Our integration with Microsoft OneDrive adheres to the Microsoft API Terms of Use. Up Cloud only accesses your OneDrive data to perform file management operations that you initiate.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your data is stored on your device and your respective cloud accounts, you retain full control over it. You can revoke Up Cloud's access to your Google or Microsoft accounts at any time from their respective security settings pages. Uninstalling Up Cloud will remove any locally stored settings and tokens.
