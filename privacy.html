Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Cloud Bucket. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our unified cloud storage manager application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Cloud Bucket acts as an interface to your existing cloud storage accounts. We collect and process the following information:

  • Authentication Information: OAuth tokens required to authenticate with Microsoft OneDrive and Google Drive.
  • Cloud Data: File metadata (names, sizes, types) and file contents retrieved strictly for the purpose of displaying and managing them within the App.
  • Local Data: Settings and preferences stored locally on your device to enhance your user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to provide the core functionality of Cloud Bucket:

  • To authenticate you with your selected cloud storage providers.
  • To retrieve, display, and manage your files (upload, download, delete, move) as explicitly directed by you.
  • We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any other unrelated purposes.

4. Data Security and Protection

We implement robust data protection mechanisms to ensure the security of your sensitive information, particularly your authentication tokens and cloud data:

  • Encryption in Transit: All communication between the Cloud Bucket application on your device and the cloud storage providers (Google and Microsoft) is encrypted using industry-standard TLS/HTTPS protocols. This ensures that your data cannot be intercepted during transfer.
  • Local Secure Storage: Cloud Bucket is a local client application. Your sensitive authentication tokens are stored securely on your local device.
  • No Remote Storage: We do not operate any backend servers that process, transmit, or store your personal files or authentication tokens. All operations happen directly between your local device and Google/Microsoft. We do not store your files or tokens on any remote Cloud Bucket servers.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy

Cloud Bucket's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, we only access Google Drive to manage your files as requested by you, and we do not share this data with third parties.

6. Microsoft API Terms

Our integration with Microsoft OneDrive adheres to the Microsoft API Terms of Use. Cloud Bucket only accesses your OneDrive data to perform file management operations that you initiate.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since your data is stored on your device and your respective cloud accounts, you retain full control over it. You can revoke Cloud Bucket's access to your Google or Microsoft accounts at any time from their respective security settings pages. Uninstalling Cloud Bucket will remove any locally stored settings and tokens.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@cloudbucket.site
